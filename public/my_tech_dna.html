02 / METHOD

Engineering thinking workflow

A practical loop that moves from field reality to durable learning.

  1. 01

    Observe

    Read the environment, constraints, symptoms, and human context.

  2. 02

    Model

    Define interfaces, dependencies, risks, and measurable outcomes.

  3. 03

    Prototype

    Build the smallest system that can challenge the assumptions.

  4. 04

    Verify

    Exercise normal, boundary, and failure paths with evidence.

  5. 05

    Encode

    Turn the lesson into automation, documentation, and the next iteration.
